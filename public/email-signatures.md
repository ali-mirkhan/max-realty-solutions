## Notes

- Always include the brokerage name and address (RECO compliance under TRESA, 2002)
- "Licensed Ontario Realtor since 1988" applies to Shahin only — other agents should use their own licensing date if they want to include it
- Do not include the fax number in regular email signatures (it adds clutter and is rarely used by recipients)
- Avoid including images/logos in the signature directly — many email clients strip or block them. Logo can be linked via URL if needed.
