## Style Guidelines

- Plain text format works best across all email clients (avoid HTML signatures with images that may render as attachments in some clients)
- Phone format: 416-XXX-XXXX (with hyphens, not parentheses)
- Always include RECO/TRESA compliance footer for legal protection
- Do not include personal social media links — keep professional
- If using a brand image, attach as a separate logo file to gmail/outlook signature settings, do NOT embed in signature text
